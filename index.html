Connection
Gemini rewrites your prompt; masked editing runs on Vertex Imagen on the server.
Mask
Paint or drag a rectangle over the area you want to edit. Painted area = will be edited.
How it works
  • Fetch flattened PNG via saveToOE('png').
  • Create transparent PNG mask (transparent = edit area).
  • Infer intent and optionally rewrite with Gemini.
  • POST to your server → Vertex AI Imagen inpainting.
  • Open the PNG result in Photopea.
